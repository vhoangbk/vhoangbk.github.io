Best Video Formats for Different Platforms

Published on January 14, 2025 | 6 min read

Choosing the right video format is crucial for optimal playback, compatibility, and file size. Each platform has specific requirements and preferences that can significantly impact your video's performance and reach.

Understanding Video Formats

Video formats consist of two main components:

  • Container: The file wrapper (MP4, AVI, MOV, etc.)
  • Codec: The compression method (H.264, H.265, VP9, etc.)

Platform-Specific Recommendations

YouTube

Recommended: MP4 with H.264 codec

  • Resolution: Up to 8K (4320p)
  • Bitrate: 8 Mbps for 1080p, 35-45 Mbps for 4K
  • Frame rate: 24, 25, 30, 50, or 60 fps
  • Audio: AAC codec, 128 kbps minimum

Facebook

Recommended: MP4 with H.264 codec

  • Resolution: 1280x720 (720p) minimum
  • Bitrate: 4 Mbps for 720p, 8 Mbps for 1080p
  • Frame rate: 30 fps maximum
  • Duration: Up to 240 minutes

Instagram

Recommended: MP4 with H.264 codec

  • Resolution: 1080x1080 (square) or 1080x1920 (vertical)
  • Bitrate: 3.5 Mbps
  • Frame rate: 30 fps
  • Duration: 15 seconds to 60 minutes

TikTok

Recommended: MP4 with H.264 codec

  • Resolution: 1080x1920 (9:16 aspect ratio)
  • Bitrate: 2-5 Mbps
  • Frame rate: 30 fps
  • Duration: 15 seconds to 10 minutes

LinkedIn

Recommended: MP4 with H.264 codec

  • Resolution: 1920x1080 (16:9 aspect ratio)
  • Bitrate: 5 Mbps
  • Frame rate: 30 fps
  • Duration: Up to 10 minutes

Professional Platforms

Vimeo

Vimeo supports high-quality uploads with minimal compression. Recommended formats include:

  • MP4 with H.264 or H.265 codec
  • ProRes 422 for professional workflows
  • Up to 8K resolution support

Twitch

For live streaming and VODs:

  • MP4 with H.264 codec
  • Resolution: 1920x1080 or 1280x720
  • Bitrate: 3-6 Mbps for 1080p

Web and Email

Web Embedding

For websites and HTML5 video players:

  • MP4 with H.264 codec (universal compatibility)
  • WebM with VP9 codec (better compression)
  • OGV format for Firefox compatibility

Email Attachments

Due to size limitations:

  • MP4 with H.264 codec
  • Resolution: 480p or 720p maximum
  • File size: Under 25MB
  • Duration: Keep under 2 minutes

Mobile Optimization

For mobile devices, consider:

  • Lower bitrates to save data
  • Adaptive bitrate streaming
  • Multiple resolution options
  • Optimized encoding profiles

Format Comparison Table

Format Compatibility File Size Quality Best For
MP4 (H.264) Excellent Medium High Universal use
MP4 (H.265) Good Small High Storage efficiency
WebM Good Small High Web optimization
MOV Limited Large Very High Professional editing

Best Practices

  1. Test compatibility across different devices and browsers
  2. Use multiple formats for maximum compatibility
  3. Optimize for your target audience and their devices
  4. Consider file size limits of your chosen platform
  5. Monitor playback performance and adjust accordingly
